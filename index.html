nanogrid

Lightweight responsive SASS/CSS grid with gutters

Inspired by Ungrid and BASSCSS I have decided to build on top of their shoulders. Welcome the nanogrid.

SASS Variables

$nanogrid-gutter: 2rem !default
$nanogrid-breakpoint: 30rem !default
